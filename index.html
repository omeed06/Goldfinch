About Us

So what is Goldfinch??


It's simple. Download Drench for free on the iOS App Store or Google Play Store.


Then, when you're out and about, you get 'drenched' with offers and exclusive discounts from restaurants, takeaways, dessert parlours and retailers, based on exactly where you are, using push notifications.


Each drench offer has a timer on it, so you have to claim the offers before they 'dry up' and disappear forever!


No more rifling through discount apps on your phone and waiting forever for some email coupon- with Drench all the offers come straight to your phone.


We made Drench to be instant, fun and surprising.
